#linear-algebra #concept
# Vectors
dot (inner) product = 0
### Sub[[vector space]]
If V and W are orthogonal, then every vector in V is perpendicular to every vector in W

# Matrices
If A is orthogonal ($U U^T = I$), then it's tranpose is its inverse ($A^T = A^{-1}$)
