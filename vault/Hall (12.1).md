#combinatorics 
Given a bipartite graph $G = (X,Y,E)$
$$\exists \, X-\text{saturating matching} \leftrightarrow \forall \, S \subseteq X, |N(S)| < |S|$$where $N(S)$ signifies neighors of vertices in $S$
