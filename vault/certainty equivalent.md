#quantitative-analysis #concept
Threshold at which you will take certain payoff over chance of a higher payoff.  Can be used in value function to bring risk tolerance into account.
- Risk averse: CE < EV
- Risk seeking: CE > EV
- Risk neutral: CE = EV
